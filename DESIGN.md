# Design System — Intercom-Inspired
# Source: https://github.com/VoltAgent/awesome-design-md/tree/main/design-md/intercom

## Colors
- Background: #faf9f6 (warm cream)
- Text: #111111 (off-black)
- Accent: #ff5600 (Fin Orange)
- Border: #dedbd6 (oat)
- Muted: #7b7b78
- Surface: #ffffff
- Dark Neutral: #313130

## Typography
- Font: Inter (sub for Saans — same geometric sans geometry)
- Headings: line-height 1.00, negative letter-spacing
- Body: line-height 1.50

## Components
- Buttons: bg #111111, text white, radius 4px, hover scale(1.1)
- Cards: bg #faf9f6, border 1px solid #dedbd6, radius 8px
- Inputs: border #dedbd6, radius 4px, focus ring orange
